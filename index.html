Our Story

A Legacy Woven
Since 1966

Sri Devi Silks was founded in 1966 by our family with a simple mission: bring the finest Indian fabrics to the families of Chennai at fair, honest prices. Over five decades, we have grown into one of K.K. Nagar's most trusted and beloved clothing stores.

Under the proprietorship of K.L. Kannan, we continue that tradition — hand-selecting every saree and garment, building lasting relationships with our customers, and celebrating the beauty of Indian textiles.
